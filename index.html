What do I consider a Highlight?

I think a highlight can be an important event but it can also be a personal event too like winning in a hard video game or winning a bet.

Here is a list of some things I might consider to be a highlight:

  • Aceing a hard test
  • Winning a race
  • Making a new friend
  • Learning something new
  • Overcoming a fear
